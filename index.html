✦ (03) — About

A studio built on craft & care.

Pixantra is a small, senior team of designers, animators and engineers. We move fast, stay personal, and obsess over the details most studios skip.

2019Founded
12Team members
4Continents served

We believe great design is invisible engineering — every pixel earning its place, every interaction paying its way.

We started Pixantra to close the gap between agencies that design and shops that build. So we do both — under one roof, with one team accountable end to end.

That means no handoffs lost in translation, no motion that dies in a spec doc, and no design that can't ship. Strategy, art direction, motion and code all live in the same room.

The result is work that feels considered at every scale — from the first fold to the last edge case.

CRAFT
Zoom in on the details

Every project gets the same obsessive attention — whether it's a global rebrand or a single hover state.

We prototype in real browsers, test on real devices, and don't call it done until it feels effortless.
